About Eaglesworld coding club

Eaglesworld coding club was created to develop future tech innovators who would in their own way shape the tech industry. Our mission is to empower students from different races with problem solving skills.

Our sole aim is to enable students understand and engage with technology and also help them develop their mental skills so as to get encouraged putting their creativity into work.

Eaglesworld coding club aims to establish a coding culture in school, making everyone passionate about coding.

Our motto: Detect the problem, analyze it and solve the problem.

OUR MISSION

We shall relentlessly provide education of excellence that meets each student interests and abilities in a safe environment which provide respect and motivate students to learn and act responsibly.


OUR VISION

To build character of determination, dedication and diligence. To bring the best out of every child. To raise excellent leaders
